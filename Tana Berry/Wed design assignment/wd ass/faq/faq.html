    • Our products have a one-year warranty

      Return to our shop, We have a professional repair shop for repairs.
      The product received is damaged

    • When you receive the package, please record the unpacking process and check if there is any problem as evidence.

      After that, you can click here to report your problem and attach your evidence.

      How to refund?

    • First, The following conditions must be met at the same time :

      1. No more than 3 days after payment
      2. Shipping has not yet started

      Then, you can click here to Customer Service for request.

      Attach the following documents:

      1. Your Full Name
      2. Your Phone Number
      3. Your E-mail
      4. Your Order Number
      5. Receipt for the Purchase of the Product
